Privacy Policy

URL, Instant Copy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

The Short Version

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything happens locally on your machine.

Permissions Explained

To be completely transparent, here is exactly why the extension requests specific permissions:

  • tabs: Used strictly to read the URL of your currently active tab so it can be copied. We do not track your browsing history.
  • offscreen & clipboardWrite: Used together to temporarily open an invisible document that writes the URL directly to your system's clipboard.

Third-Party Services

There are no third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or external servers connected to this extension. It operates entirely offline.
